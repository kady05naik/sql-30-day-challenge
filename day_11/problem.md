## 🎯 Objective  

As a Data Engineer supporting a university mental health research team, your task is to analyze the psychological well-being of international students based on how long they have stayed in the host country.

The goal is to:

- Segment international students by duration of stay  
- Measure key mental health indicators:
  - Depression score (PHQ)
  - Social Connectedness score (SCS)
  - Acculturative Stress score (AS)  
- Calculate aggregated metrics for each stay category  
- Round numerical outputs for reporting accuracy  
- Present insights in a structured format suitable for dashboards and research reporting  

This analysis helps stakeholders understand how duration of stay impacts student well-being and supports data-driven decisions for improving student support programs.
